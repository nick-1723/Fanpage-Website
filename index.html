Background On Website:

The purpose of this fanpage is to illustrate the good influences and the examples that the music industry leaves behind. Music is a way for one to express their emotions through a form of music just like painters and writers. These singers and rappers do their job in such a way that is their own and unique to the way they rap/sing.
